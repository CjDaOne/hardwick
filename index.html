welcome to HARDWICK'S WOOD CANDLES

My story hello I’m Ta’Nece, I had my greatest experience in May of 2021!
Smelling the aroma of my favorite candle walking down the aisle staring into my future husband eyes, one of my favorite moments.
From that moment, every time I smell that scent it gives me nostalgia moment.
My goal is for my candles to be the bridge between you and your nostalgic moments through an amazing aroma.
Becoming Mrs Hardwick gave me the perfect name for my candle business. So I welcome you to Hardwick's Wood Candles, by Ta'nece!
